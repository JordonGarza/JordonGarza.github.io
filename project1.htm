REU on Smart UAV's at Auburn University

This project during Summer 2018 focuses on devloping a method of travel and homing on a UAV drone without the use of GPS. The method that is being developed is a use of stereo vision and built-in cameras in an AR. Drone 2.0 in order to recognize and log a "home base" physical marker that the drone will be able to autonomously travel to when the drone is finished with an arbitrary mission of movement away from the marker.
